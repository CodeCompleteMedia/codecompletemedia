A fund's website is its handshake. Before a single call happens, prospective LPs
and founders judge whether the team looks serious, organized, and worth their
time. Entre needed a site that did that work on the first scroll.

## The challenge

Entre had a strong thesis and a weak web presence — a generic template that
undersold the team and buried the things that actually build confidence: track
record, focus, and clarity about how they work.

## What we did

We rebuilt the site around credibility. A restrained, editorial design signals
seriousness without shouting. The structure puts the thesis and proof points up
top, and every page loads fast enough that the experience itself reads as
competence.

> The site finally matches how the team actually operates — sharp and clear.

## The outcome

Entre now opens conversations from a position of strength. The site loads in
well under a second, scores in the high 90s on Lighthouse, and gives the team
something they're glad to send before a first meeting instead of apologizing for.
