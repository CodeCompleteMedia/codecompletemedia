A personal brand should open doors before you walk through them. The goal here
was simple: a site that makes the right people want to reply.

## The challenge

Plenty of accomplishments, no single place that tied them together. The story
was scattered across profiles and PDFs, and none of it sounded like an actual
person.

## What we did

We led with voice. The copy was rewritten to sound like a real conversation, and
the design got out of its way — generous type, clear hierarchy, and a single
obvious next step on every screen.

## The outcome

The site now works as an introduction. It reads quickly, sounds human, and gives
people an immediate reason to reach out instead of moving on.
